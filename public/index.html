About Sevika

Sevika is a hyper-local charity platform that enables individuals and organizations to donate surplus clothes, food, medicines, and essential items to nearby charities and collection centers.

The platform ensures that all donated items undergo verification before collection and distribution to maintain safety, quality, and trust.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To reduce the wastage of surplus essential items by providing a simple and reliable digital platform that connects donors with people in need, promoting responsible sharing and community support.

Vision

To create a sustainable, technology-driven system that encourages efficient redistribution of essential resources and strengthens community welfare through transparent and ethical practices.

How It Works

Donate

Donors list surplus clothes, food, medicines, or essential items.

Verification

Medicines are checked for expiry and other items are inspected for quality.

Collect & Distribute

Verified items are collected and distributed to people in need.

Verification & Safety

Medicine Safety

Ensures medicines are not expired and safe for consumption.

Item Quality

Food, clothes, and essentials are checked for acceptable condition.

Trusted Distribution

Only verified donations are approved for final distribution.
